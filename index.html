How to Use

Request Feature / Feedback

Upload Your Data

Drag & drop a CSV or Excel file (up to 49MB) into the left sidebar, or click to select a file from your device.

Ask for Insights & Visualizations

Use the chat to ask questions about your data. The AI will provide text summaries and generate interactive charts. For example: "Show total sales by region as a bar chart".

Transform & Clean Data

Request changes to your dataset using natural language. This permanently modifies your current session's data. Example: "Remove all rows where profit is less than 0".

Filter with a Double-Click

Double-click on any bar, pie slice, or donut segment in a chart. This will stage a filter in the sidebar. Apply it to analyze a specific subset of your data.

Export Your Work

Use the buttons in the sidebar to download the current state of your data (original or filtered) as a CSV or Excel file. You can also export individual charts as PNG images.
